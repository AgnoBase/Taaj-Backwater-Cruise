About Us

Experience the serenity of Poovar on a breathtaking boating cruise

Neyyattinkara's Poovar Island, which is located in the Thiruvananthapuram district, is a popular tourist site. Situated at Kerala's most southern point, it is close to Vizhinjam, a natural harbour. When the tides are higher than usual, Poovar has an estuary that connects to the sea. One of Kerala's most stunning locations is Poovar. The backwaters in Poovar, Kerala are what give the city its attractiveness. At Kerala's poovar, Poovar Boating provides a highly dependable boating service.
